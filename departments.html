Computer Engineering

Shaping the Digital Future

The Department of Computer Engineering at SRTTC is committed to providing quality education in the rapidly evolving field of computer science and technology.

Our department offers comprehensive programs that blend theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students to excel in software development, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

B.E. Bachelor of Engineering
Diploma 3 Year Program

  • Industry-aligned curriculum covering AI, ML, Cloud Computing, IoT
  • Expert faculty with industry experience and research background
  • Strong industry partnerships with TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and more
  • Regular workshops, seminars, and technical competitions

Mechanical Engineering

Innovation in Manufacturing and Design

The Mechanical Engineering department combines traditional mechanical principles with modern technologies to prepare students for diverse industrial challenges.

Our program emphasizes hands-on learning through well-equipped workshops and laboratories, ensuring students gain practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge in design, manufacturing, thermal sciences, and automation.

Programs Offered

B.E. Bachelor of Engineering
Diploma 3 Year Program

Key Highlights

  • Advanced CAD/CAM/CAE laboratories
  • CNC machines and modern manufacturing equipment
  • Experienced faculty with industry expertise
  • Strong industry collaborations
  • Project-based learning approach
  • Regular industrial visits and internships
  • Active participation in SAE India activities
  • Focus on sustainable and green technologies

Specializations & Focus Areas

  • Computer Aided Design (CAD) and Manufacturing (CAM)
  • Thermal Engineering and Energy Systems
  • Manufacturing Processes and Automation
  • Robotics and Mechatronics
  • Automobile Engineering
  • Industrial Engineering and Management
  • Renewable Energy Technologies
  • Materials Science and Engineering
