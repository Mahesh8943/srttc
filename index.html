About SRTTC

Nagar Yuvak Shikshan Sanstha's Suman Ramesh Tulsiani Technical Campus - Faculty of Engineering is a premier institution committed to providing quality technical education.

Located in the scenic locale of Khamshet, Pune, our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern laboratories, and excellent facilities that foster an environment conducive to learning and innovation.

Approved by AICTE and DTE Maharashtra, and affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) and Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), we offer comprehensive engineering programs that prepare students for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Our institution is proud to be ISO 9001:2015 certified and accredited by NAAC with 'B+' Grade, reflecting our commitment to maintaining high standards of education and continuous improvement.
